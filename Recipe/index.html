Japanese Noodle Salad

About this recipe

This delicious and refreshing salad is perfect for hot summer days!

Hide Photos

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces vermicelli pasta
  • 6 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 4 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 cucumber
  • green onions

Directions

  • 1. Cook noodles in boiling water until tender.
  • 2. Drain and rinse the noodles in cold water, then transfer to a bowl.
  • 3. Add vinegar, sugar, soy sauce, and sesame seeds.
  • 4. Mix well. Cover and chill until cold.
  • 5. Peel and cut the cucumber into quarters length, then slice into thin pieces.
  • 6. Chop the green onion into small pieces.
  • 7. Top noodles with cucumber slices, and sprinkle with sesame seeds and chopped green onion.
